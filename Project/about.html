About Kiddie Tech-ville

At Kiddie Tech-ville, we believe in making coding and robotics education accessible and affordable for children across Africa.
We partner with schools to create tech clubs that inspire and engage students.
We are passionate about equipping the next generation with the skills they need to succeed in the digital world.
We provide robotics hardware kits with affordable, flexible installment plans for accessibility.
And mobile-optimized, bite-sized lessons as well as interactive coding environments for seamless learning on the go.
Through our online classes, we bring quality tech education right to your home.
Our holiday classes provide intensive, hands-on learning experiences that make technology fun and exciting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a generation of innovators and problem solvers who will drive Africa forward.
